attention

Every word looks
at every other word.

This is the whole trick behind the transformer. To read one word, the model scores how much every other word matters to it. Pick a word and watch where its attention goes: "it" knows it means the animal, not the street.

sampling

One knob decides
safe or strange.

The model produces a score for every possible next token. Temperature decides how sharply those scores turn into a choice. Turn it down and the model repeats itself; turn it up and it starts to wander.
